SERVICES

Our hospital is equipped with various departments, each specializing in a specific area of healthcare. Here is an overview of the departments you can find in our hospital:

General Medicine/Internal Medicine

This department focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of general medical conditions, providing primary healthcare services.

Surgery

The surgical department specializes in performing a wide range of surgical procedures, including general surgery, orthopedic surgery, cardiovascular surgery, neurosurgery, and more.

Cardiology

This department deals with heart-related conditions and offers diagnostic tests, non-invasive procedures, and interventions for heart diseases.

Oncology

The oncology department is dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of various types of cancers, providing chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgical interventions.

Neurology

This department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of disorders related to the nervous system, including conditions like epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis, and neurodegenerative diseases.

Pediatrics

The pediatric department specializes in the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents, providing vaccinations, routine check-ups, and treatment for various pediatric conditions.

Obstetrics and Gynecology

This department offers comprehensive care for women's reproductive health, including prenatal care, childbirth, gynecological surgeries, and treatment for reproductive disorders.

Orthopedics

The orthopedic department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal conditions, including bone fractures, joint replacements, and sports injuries.

Emergency Medicine

This department provides immediate medical care for acute illnesses, injuries, and emergencies, ensuring timely and critical care for patients in need.
